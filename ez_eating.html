EZ Eating

EZ Eating is a responsive recipe web application that specializes in simplifying everyday cooking.

My Process

Research

Prior to designing, I researched different competing apps. The two I focused on were Delish and Kitchen Stories.
Kitchen Stories focuses on encouraging the statement "anyone can cook". Delish focuses on those who really like to cook and those who really like to eat.
After spending time on both of these apps, I was able to create an app that took all the pros of numerous apps and apply it to one.

User Flow

The user flow includes and allows:
  • Creation or sign in of an account
  • Setting preferences for dietary limitations
  • Search of recipes
  • Adjusting portion sizes

Style Guide

  • The color palette used is to produce a natural and healthy feeling
  • The typography chosen was used to present a simple and easy read
