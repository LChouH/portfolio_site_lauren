Hidden Gems

Hidden Gems is simple way to find something new to watch that makes suggestions based on streaming services you already pay for. Search different shows to find watch options.

User Flow

The user flow includes and allows:
  • Creation or sign in of an account
  • Selection of streaming subscriptions
  • Search of movies and tv shows
  • Usage of "Gem Finder" that allows the user to find new shows
